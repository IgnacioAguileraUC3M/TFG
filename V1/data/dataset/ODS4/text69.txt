

      More than half of children aged 3 and 4 in selected countries are developmentally on track in physical  learning and psycho-social well-being
    

    Early childhood development is multidimensional  encompassing several aspects of a childs well-being: physical  social  emotional and mental. Despite variations in the pace and rate at which children develop  all children have an inherent right to develop to their fullest potential.   In 54 of 58 countries with available data for the period 20092015  at least half of children aged 3 and 4 were developmentally on track in at least three of the following domains: literacy-numeracy  physical development  social-emotional development  and learning.
